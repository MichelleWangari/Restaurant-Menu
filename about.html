About Us

Restaurant's History

The Gilded Table is a fine establishment that has been up and running since 2015.Founded by the Jonas brothers it has developed into a luxurious location that offers an exquisite and unique dining experience.Here we take pride in serving gourmet dishes crafted with the freshest ingredients and utmost passion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer an unforgettable dining experience by blending exceptional flavours, top-tier service and an elegant ambience.We do believe that food is an art!
